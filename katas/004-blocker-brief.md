# The Blocker Brief

You need to communicate a technical constraint to a non-engineer.

- Write it before you send it
- Sentence 1: _What_
- Sentence 2: _Why_
- Sentence 3: _Options_
- Remove any word they would need to look up

**If they cannot act on what you said, you did not communicate.**
